Arepas 101

Ingredients

Dough

  • Pre-cooked Corn Flower
  • Water
  • Salt

Filling

  • Protien of Choice (G. Beef)
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Vegetables (Carrots, Beans, Corn...)

How to Make Arepas

  1. Mix flower, water, and salt in bowl
  2. Knead with hands till firm
  3. If cracking, add more water
  4. If soggy, add more flower
  5. Make filling, either via skillet or oven
  6. Season to taste
  7. Knead dough into palm-sized-paddies
  8. Place corn paddy into a pan about an inch thick high of oil
  9. Fry each side for about 5 - 8 minutes
  10. Cut fried paddy in half with butter knife, insert filling
  11. Enjoy!
