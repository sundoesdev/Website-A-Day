Multiply by 11 Fast Technique

Description

Any number multiplied by 11 can be solved very quickly in your mind with a cheap trick.

Here are the steps for doing it:

Steps

  1. Write the unit-digit and end-digit on both ends of the product
  2. The sum of both those numbers goes in the middle (?)
  3. Concatenate everything: (end-digit) + (sum) + (unit-digit)
  4. Done!

Example

  1. 36 * 11 = ?
  2. 36 --> 3 (?) 6
  3. 3 + 6 = 9
  4. (?) = 9
  5. 3 (?) 6 = 396
  6. 36 * 11 = 396!

3-Digit Example

  1. 772 * 11 = (?)
  2. 7 (?) 2
  3. 7 + 7 = 14
  4. 8 (?) 2
  5. 7 + 2 = 9
  6. 772 * 11 = 8492!
