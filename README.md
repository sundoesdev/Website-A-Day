# A Website A Day Keeps the Doctor Away

## What Is This?
Wanna upgrade your skills as a developer but don't know how?: Make a website. Every, single, day. (Without frameworks!)

I really do consider development and programming to be some kind of botched form of art with heavy ties to math and the abstract world. An art that I wish to improve at, 1% everyday.

Web-development is a great way to sustain daily practice that can also be artistic, while still gaining skills that can be applied in whatever it is you do as a developer. The origin and history of the internet hinges on one simple and beautiful product: The humble website.

I do it for the [love of the game](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/for_the_love_of_the_game).

One Day, or, Day One.

## Goals
- Over 100 consecutive days, make 100 websites
- Improve as a developer, 1% everyday; learn something new
- Take my newfound skills to my other areas of interests: RC embedded systems via websockets, Making desktop apps with Electron, etc...
